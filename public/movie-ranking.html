My All Time Favorite Movies

Top 5 Movies of All Time

These are the best movies I've watched this year, and I highly recommend them to anyone who hasn't seen them yet!

1. Interstellar 🚀👩‍🚀

A mind-bending sci-fi adventure that explores space, time, and love. Directed by Christopher Nolan, this film takes you on an emotional journey through the cosmos.

2. How to Train Your Dragon 🐉👱

An animated masterpiece that tells the story of a young Viking who befriends a dragon. It's a heartwarming tale of friendship and courage.

3. Midnight 🚨🔪

A tense Korean thriller where a deaf woman becomes the target of a relentless serial killer, It's a gripping Korean movie that'll keep you on the edge all night!

4. Howl's Moving Castle 🏰🧙‍♂️

A magical animated film by Studio Ghibli that follows a young woman transformed into an old lady by a witches curse. It's a beautifully animated movies with stunning visuals. I also recommend other Studio Ghibli films like Spirited Away and My Neighbor Totoro.

5. M3GAN 🤖👧

M3GAN is a sci-fi horror thriller about a lifelike AI doll designed to be a child's best freind and protector, Trust me Megan is wild.
